​Group Competition
​
Category
Patriotic 
(Preferably in Hindi/ Marathi)
Lyrics submission is required for non-Hindi/ Marathi.

Eligibility
  • Classes VII to IX
  • 18  student participants Including accompanists.
  • Only ONE group from each school can participate.
  
​Accompaniment
  • Only live instruments would be allowed.
  • Music played by the participating students will be  highly encouraged.
  • Backing music in any form will not be accepted.
  • It is mandatory for participants to bring their own instruments.
   
​Attire
Participants are encouraged to wear cultural or traditional attire that aligns with the theme and origin of the song.

​ Time Limit
  • Mic and stage set up time: 3 minutes
  • Performance: 5 to 7 minutes
  • Participants should abide by the given time limit.

Disqualification 
  • Any form of plagiarism or use of pre-recorded tracks.
  • Exceeding group size, inappropriate content.
  • Negative marking for exceeding time limit.

​Solo Competition

​
Category
Sugam Sangeet
(Preferably in Hindi/ Marathi)  
Lyrics submission is required for
non-Hindi/ Marathi songs.

 Eligibility 
  • Classes IV to VI
  • Only ONE student with two accompanists from each school can participate.

​Accompaniment
  • Live instruments (like harmonium, tabla, dholak etc) can be used.
  • Teachers can accompany the student.

Song Selection
  • Songs must fall under Sugam Sangeet (Light classical, Bhavgeet, Gawlan, Abhang, Bhakti Geet, with classical base). ​ 

Attire
​Participants are encouraged to wear cultural or traditional attire that aligns with the theme and origin of the song.
​
Time Limit 
  • Mic and stage set up time: 3 minutes
  • Performance time: 3 to 5 minutes
  • ​A participant  should abide by the given time limit.
 
​Disqualification 
  • Any form of plagiarism or use of pre-recorded tracks. 
  • Inappropriate content.
  • Negative marking for exceeding time limit.
